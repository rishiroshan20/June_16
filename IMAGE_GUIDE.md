## üìê Image Specifications

### Ideal Image Properties

**Orientation**: Portrait (vertical) is BEST
- Width: 600-1000 pixels
- Height: 800-1500 pixels
- Aspect Ratio: 3:4 or 9:16

**Format**: JPG or JPEG (PNG also works)

**File Size**: Keep under 500KB each
- Faster loading
- Smooth animations
- Better performance

**Quality**: High resolution but optimized
- Clear and sharp
- Good lighting
- Well-composed

## üé® Photo Tips for Best Results

### Lighting
- Choose well-lit photos
- Natural lighting is best
- Avoid dark or grainy images

### Composition
- Clear subjects (you both should be visible)
- Avoid cluttered backgrounds if possible
- Emotional expressions are perfect

### Editing (Optional)
- Slight brightness/contrast adjustments are fine
- Keep it natural - don't over-edit
- The website adds a cinematic filter automatically

## üîß How to Optimize Your Images

### Using Online Tools (Free & Easy)

1. **TinyPNG** (tinypng.com)
   - Upload your image
   - Automatically compresses
   - Download optimized version

2. **Squoosh** (squoosh.app)
   - More control over compression
   - See before/after comparison
   - Multiple format options

3. **iLoveIMG** (iloveimg.com)
   - Resize and compress
   - Batch processing
   - Simple interface

### Using Your Phone

**iPhone**:
1. Open photo in Photos app
2. Tap Edit
3. Adjust lighting/crop if needed
4. Tap Done
5. Share > Save to Files

**Android**:
1. Open photo in Gallery
2. Tap Edit
3. Adjust as needed
4. Save copy
5. Use Google Photos to compress
